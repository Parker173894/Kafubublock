KAFUBU PORTAL VERSION 5 - PUPIL RESULT LOOKUP
==============================================

- Added a Check Pupil Results page at /pupil-results.
- Pupils can enter their student number and full registered name.
- Both details must match the same pupil record before results are displayed.
- Names are matched without being affected by capital letters or extra spaces.
- Added links from the homepage, navigation and portal login page.
- The Headteacher, Deputy Headteacher or HR can continue opening and closing
  pupil result access from the existing Pupil Portal Control page.
- Repeated wrong searches are limited for 15 minutes.
- Public result pages are not cached and are excluded from search indexing.
- Staff result entry, analysis and authenticated pupil accounts are unchanged.
