KAFUBU PORTAL VERSION 3 UPGRADE NOTES
=====================================

VISUAL DESIGN
- Added a modern responsive design across public pages and portal screens.
- Added a sticky school header, mobile navigation, polished cards, forms,
  tables, buttons, alerts, profiles and footer.
- Redesigned the homepage and academic department pages.

AUTOMATIC DEPARTMENT RECORDS
- Every HOD registration is automatically assigned to that HOD's department.
- A submitted form cannot move the teacher to another department.
- New teachers appear immediately on the correct public department page.
- When a teacher edits the profile, the department staff card updates from
  the same live database record.
- The related staff-return record is synchronised at the same time.
- Management profile edits and department reassignments are also synchronised.
- Inactive Subject Teacher accounts are hidden from public department pages.

HOW TO TEST
1. Login as an HOD using Other Portal Login.
2. Select Register New Subject Teacher.
3. Confirm the department is displayed automatically and cannot be changed.
4. Complete registration and open View Department Staff Page.
5. Logout and use Teacher Login with the new credentials.
6. Change the temporary password, edit the teacher profile and save.
7. Open View My Department Profile and confirm the new details are displayed.
